1ffb: Jump to the address in hl and reset DivMMC mapper
Used by the routines at normalInit, L0c1d, L0c88, L0ce4 and L0d65.
L1ffb 1ffb jp (hl) Jump to the address held in hl. Executing an instruction at this address also causes the DivMMC mapper to be reset meaning the 48K is now mapped-in.
