00df: Map 48K ROM
The upper bit of the ROM selection is in bit 2 of the ZX Spectrum +2A/+3 page control register at $1ffd and the lower bit is in bit 4 of the ZX Spectrum 128 page control register at $7ffd.
This routine works on all Spectrums >= 128K
ROM selection is as follows:
  • 00b: 128k editor, menu system and self-test program
  • 01b: 128k syntax checker
  • 10b: +3DOS
  • 11b: 48K BASIC
Used by the routine at init.
map48ROM 00df ld bc,$1ffd ZX Spectrum +2A/+3 page control register
00e2 ld a,$04 Upper bit of ROM selection
00e4 out (c),a
00e6 ld b,$7f ZX Spectrum 128 page control register
00e8 ld a,$10 Lower bit of ROM selection
00ea out (c),a
00ec ret
