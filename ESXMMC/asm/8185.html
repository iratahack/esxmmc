1ff9: Enable interrupts, return and reset DivMMC mapper
L1ff9 1ff9 ei
This entry point is used by the routines at L005b, L0d14 and L1ff4.
L1ff9_0 1ffa ret Executing an instruction at this address causes the DivMMC mapper to be reset meaning the 48K is now mapped-in.
